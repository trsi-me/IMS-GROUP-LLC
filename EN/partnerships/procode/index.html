Professional Partnership with procode UAE

Professional Partnership • Feb 1, 2026 | 18 Sha'ban 1447 H

We are proud to announce today, Feb 1, 2026 (18 Sha'ban 1447 H), the signing of a strategic professional partnership agreement between our company, Ibn Mubarak AL-Shahrani Group LLC, represented by our official affiliated personal brand "Tler AL-Shahrani," and procode UAE. This step transforms the relationship from professional competition to professional cooperation based on integration and specialization, reflecting mature vision and achieving the strategic objectives of both brands while enhancing their market position.

This partnership stems from a shared vision to expand the scope of technical services provided to students and clients, particularly in the field of technical and engineering graduation projects that require actual integration between software and hardware solutions, as well as innovative business projects, especially technical projects and startups.

Under this agreement, the first party—our company—represented by CEO Mohammed AL-Shahrani, is responsible for software and academic aspects, including software development, contract management, client communication, and full supervision of the technical and software component of projects.

In return, the second party—procode—represented by Relations and Meetings Officer and authorized signatory Salma Mohammed, is responsible for executing technical and engineering aspects related to hardware and explaining them to clients when necessary, relying on the technical expertise and advanced operational capabilities of their distinguished team.

This partnership affirms the principle of professional role division, whereby each party operates within its scope of specialization, with a shared commitment to the highest quality standards, adherence to timelines, protection of both parties' reputations, and ensuring projects are delivered in an integrated and secure manner that meets client expectations.

The agreement also stipulates the organization of financial relations, preservation of intellectual property rights, and full commitment to professional confidentiality, reflecting the level of institutional maturity and governance in managing technical professional partnerships.

This partnership represents a practical model of how cooperation based on trust and specialization can create real and sustainable value, enhance the presence of both brands in the Saudi and UAE markets, and support the growing demand for integrated technical solutions.
