Who We Are

About the Company

A management company that organizes, manages, and develops exclusively our affiliated brands and business partnerships. We do not sell products or services directly to customers; all sales and services are conducted exclusively through our affiliated brands. We operate as a central management entity that manages affiliated brands and provides them with comprehensive administrative, strategic, financial, technical, and marketing support. The group was officially established from the date of its commercial registration on December 3, 2025, as the second establishment after being "Tler Group LLC" since August 27, 2025. We are a digital company operating primarily in Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Our Programs

Available Programs

Affiliate Marketing Program

A specialized program that enables individuals to join the team and earn commissions for their marketing efforts. It was established on August 13, 2025. The program is open to all nationalities and ages from 13 years and above, and users can register and participate with ease. The program operates under the management and supervision of the group, specifically the Business Management Department, and provides a comprehensive system for tracking commissions and rewards. Commission rates range from 3-10% with a flexible commission increase mechanism linked to the marketer's performance, and there are performance bonuses (Bonus) for outstanding marketers, making it one of the best affiliate marketing programs in the region.

Program Features :

  • Transparent and fair commission system (3-10%)
  • Instant profit transfer upon completion of sale
  • A flexible commission rate increase mechanism linked to the marketer's performance

  • Easy registration and participation
  • Comprehensive free training

Other Departments

Technology Department

Programming, Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence

Handles the development of innovative technical solutions, protecting systems and networks from cyber threats, and developing artificial intelligence software. It includes a team of programmers, developers, and cybersecurity and artificial intelligence experts who work to support brands and clients with advanced technical solutions in all forms, from software tools, websites, web and mobile applications, and artificial intelligence models built from scratch.

Legal Department

Handles supervision of all legal aspects and compliance with local and international regulations. It ensures protection of the company's and its brands' legal interests, manages all contracts including agreements, partnerships, and non-disclosure agreements (NDA), and provides necessary legal consultations. The department works to ensure full compliance with laws and regulations in force in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Sales & Marketing

Handles managing sales and digital marketing strategies for brands. It develops effective marketing campaigns, manages customer relationships, and analyzes market performance to improve results and increase sales. The department works on developing comprehensive marketing strategies including digital marketing, content creation, social media management, and performance analysis.

Finance and Accounting

Handles managing financial and accounting affairs of the company and its brands. It prepares financial reports and statements, manages budgets, monitors and periodically improves financial performance, and ensures compliance with financial and tax laws. The department works on managing financial accounts, preparing all periodic financial reports, managing budgets, and ensuring compliance with applicable financial and tax laws.

Human Resources

Handles managing employee affairs, recruitment, and professional development. It ensures availability of qualified personnel, manages recruitment processes, and develops training and development programs for employees. The department works on attracting talent, managing recruitment processes, developing employees, and ensuring a professional and motivating work environment for all employees.

Business Management

Handles strategic planning and managing daily operations of the group. It coordinates between different departments, monitors project implementation, and ensures efficient achievement of strategic goals. The department works on developing strategic plans, monitoring their implementation, and coordinating between all departments to ensure achievement of desired goals.

Project Management

Handles managing and organizing various projects for the group and its brands. It coordinates between different resources and teams, monitors project progress, and ensures completion on time and within the allocated budget. The department works on developing project plans, monitoring their implementation, and managing risks to ensure project success and achievement of desired goals.

Creativity and Innovation

Handles developing innovative ideas and visual and creative solutions for the group, its brands, and their clients. It works on exploring new opportunities, developing innovative products and services, and improving current processes. The department works on encouraging an innovation culture, developing new ideas, and transforming them into practical solutions that add real value to brands and clients.

Daily Operations

Handles managing and organizing daily operations of the group and its brands. It ensures smooth and efficient workflow, monitors daily tasks, and maintains the quality of services provided. The department works on improving daily operations, ensuring compliance with standards and quality, and coordinating work between different departments to ensure continuous services with high efficiency.

Customer Service

Handles providing technical support to clients and resolving technical issues. It ensures service continuity, responds to client inquiries, and maintains client satisfaction through excellent customer service. The department works on providing continuous technical support to clients, resolving technical issues promptly and efficiently, and ensuring client satisfaction with the services provided.
