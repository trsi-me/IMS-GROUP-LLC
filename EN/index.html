Who We Are

About the Group

An administrative company that organizes, manages, and develops exclusively our affiliated brands and business partnerships. We do not sell products or services directly to clients; all sales and services are conducted exclusively through our affiliated brands. We operate as a central management that manages affiliated brands and provides them with comprehensive administrative, strategic, financial, technical, and marketing support. The group was officially established from the date of its commercial registration on Dec 3, 2025, as the second establishment after being "Tler Group LLC" since Aug 27, 2025, which was completely and permanently closed by the owner due to administrative and legal issues. We are a digital company operating primarily in Jeddah, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

What We Want to Convey and Achieve

Our Vision

To be the first administrative partner for ambitious brands in the region. We strive to be the primary reference in the strategic management and development of brands through integrated administrative, technical, and marketing solutions that enable brands to achieve sustainable growth and excellence in the local and regional markets. We aspire to be pioneers in brand management and to serve as a model to follow in the region, with a focus on supporting our nationals and employing creative Saudi talent and developing them professionally as a fundamental part of our strategy. As part of our commitment to the principles of sustainable development, we believe that empowering women and supporting their professional participation is a foundational pillar in building a leading and sustainable institution.

Our Mission

Empowering brands through professional management and innovative technical and marketing solutions. We operate as a strategic partner supporting brands on their journey to success and excellence in the local and regional markets. We seek to deliver real value to brands through integrated management covering all administrative, technical, and marketing aspects, ensuring their sustainable growth and success in the market, with special attention to empowering national talent and developing them professionally. We also pay special attention to empowering women and developing them professionally, as an integral part of our ecosystem for empowering national talent.

Available Programs and Initiatives

Affiliate Marketing Program

A specialized program that enables individuals to join the team and earn commissions for their marketing efforts. It was established on Aug 13, 2025. The program is open to all nationalities and ages from 13 years and above, and users can register and participate with ease. The program operates under the management and supervision of the group, specifically the Business Management Department, and provides a comprehensive system for tracking commissions and rewards. Commission rates range from 3-10% with a flexible commission increase mechanism linked to the marketer's performance, and there are performance bonuses (Bonus) for outstanding marketers, making it one of the best affiliate marketing programs in the region.

Group Departments

Senior Management

Senior management is the administrative and strategic heart of the group. It operates in an executive capacity and is responsible for defining future visions, formulating strategies and plans, allocating resources, and ensuring the success of all affiliated brands and business partnerships. Management supervises all departments and coordinates operations across the group's various divisions. It is responsible for making major decisions and attracting key clients from merchants, establishments, and government entities to ensure sustainable growth and continued excellence for all brands and partnerships. Senior management comprises experienced leaders across all group departments and sectors, most notably in management, marketing, development and digital transformation, and governance.

Technology Department

Programming, Cybersecurity, AI, Networks

Handles the development of innovative technical solutions, protecting systems and networks from cyber threats, and developing AI software. It includes a team of programmers, developers, and cybersecurity and AI experts who work to support brands and clients with advanced technical solutions in all forms, from software tools, websites, web and mobile applications, and AI models built from scratch.

Legal Department

Handles supervision of all legal aspects and ensuring compliance with local and international regulations. It ensures protection of the group's, its brands', and clients' legal interests, manages all contracts including service agreements, partnerships, and non-disclosure agreements (NDA), provides necessary legal consultations, and bears full responsibility for following up on cases and representing the group in legal proceedings. The department also works to ensure full compliance with laws and regulations in force in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Sales & Marketing

Handles managing and developing sales and digital marketing strategies for brands. It manages marketing campaigns effectively, manages client relationships, and analyzes market performance periodically to improve results and increase sales and sustainable growth with maximum efficiency, lowest costs, and fastest time. The department works on developing comprehensive content and marketing strategies including digital marketing, content creation, social media management, marketing or advertising campaign management, and performance analysis.

Finance and Accounting

Handles managing financial and accounting affairs of the group and its brands. It prepares financial reports and statements, manages budgets, monitors and periodically improves financial performance, and ensures compliance with financial and tax laws. The department works on managing financial accounts, preparing all periodic financial reports, managing budgets, and ensuring compliance with applicable financial and tax laws.

HR

Handles managing employee affairs, recruitment, and professional development. It ensures availability of qualified personnel, manages recruitment processes, and develops training and development programs for employees. The department works on attracting talent, managing recruitment processes, developing employees, and ensuring a professional, flexible, and motivating work environment for all employees.

Business Management

Handles strategic planning and managing daily operations of the group. It coordinates between different departments, monitors project implementation, and ensures efficient achievement of strategic goals. The department works on developing strategic plans, monitoring their implementation, and coordinating between all departments to ensure achievement of desired goals.

Project Management

Handles managing and organizing various projects for the group and its brands. It coordinates between different resources and teams, monitors project progress, and ensures completion on time and within the allocated budget. The department works on developing project plans, monitoring their implementation, and managing risks to ensure project success and achievement of desired goals.

Creativity and Innovation

Handles developing innovative ideas and visual and creative solutions for the group, its brands, and their clients. It works on exploring new opportunities, developing innovative products and services, and improving current processes. The department works on encouraging an innovation culture, developing new ideas, and transforming them into practical solutions that add real value to brands and clients.

Daily Operations

Handles managing and organizing daily operations of the group and its brands. It ensures smooth and efficient workflow, monitors daily tasks, and maintains the quality of services provided. The department works on improving daily operations, ensuring compliance with standards and quality, and coordinating work between different departments to ensure continuous services with high efficiency.

Client Service

Handles providing technical support to clients and resolving technical issues. It ensures service continuity, responds to client inquiries, and maintains client satisfaction through excellent client service. The department works on providing continuous technical support to clients, resolving technical issues promptly and efficiently, and ensuring client satisfaction with the services provided.
